# 🔥 News
- [***2026.05***] We are thrilled to announce the **[Extreme Driving Dataset](https://sean-shiyuez.github.io/extreme-driving-dataset-web/)**! Unlike conventional datasets, this multi-modal collection is explicitly designed to tackle **long-tail challenges and limit-handling**. It features 6 demanding scenarios—including high-G emergency maneuvers, massive traction loss in snow, and severely degraded perception in rain/low-light. Supported by high-fidelity chassis signals (raw IMU, absolute GPS yaw), it provides the ultimate testbed for robust VLA models and extreme vehicle dynamics.<br>**-Project Page:** [https://sean-shiyuez.github.io/extreme-driving-dataset-web/](https://sean-shiyuez.github.io/extreme-driving-dataset-web/)<br>**-Dataset URL:** To be released with the arXiv preprint within 1 month.
